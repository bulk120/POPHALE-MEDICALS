About POPHALE MEDICALS

Established with a commitment to community health, POPHALE MEDICALS has been serving the residents of Sangamner and surrounding areas for years. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality pharmaceutical care.

Located at a convenient location opposite B.Ed. College on Akole Bypass Road, we are easily accessible to all. Our multi-story facility ensures we have ample space to serve you with a wide range of healthcare products and services.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through trust, transparency, and exceptional service.
